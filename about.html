About Our Company

KAYO PLC

Founded in 2003, KAYO PLC is a premier family-owned company that has established a significant presence in Ethiopia’s confectionery industry. Renowned for our dedication to quality and innovation, KAYO has become a leading name through our flagship brand, Meaza, known for its premium confectionery products.


State-of-the-Art Facilities

KAYO operates two cutting-edge production facilities in Ethiopia, covering 1000 and 800 square meters. Both facilities are equipped with the latest machinery and operated by a team of skilled professionals who ensure our products meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

Commitment to Quality

Quality is the cornerstone of KAYO’s operations. Our dedicated team meticulously supervises each stage of production to ensure that every Meaza product fulfills the exacting standards our customers expect. This relentless pursuit of excellence has made Meaza a trusted and beloved name in Ethiopian households.

Innovation and Growth

Innovation drives our business strategy at KAYO. We are continually exploring new ways to refine our existing products and introduce exciting new options. Our forward-thinking approach enables us to look beyond local markets, with ambitions to introduce Meaza’s delightful flavors to the global stage. By embracing new methodologies and staying responsive to market trends, KAYO aims to set new standards in the confectionery industry.

Wide Reach

Our comprehensive distribution network guarantees that Meaza products are accessible across Ethiopia, from bustling cities to remote villages. We are committed to ensuring that every customer can enjoy the exceptional taste and quality of Meaza confectioneries.

Vision for the Future

KAYO is poised for continued growth and innovation. We aspire to expand into international markets, sharing the unique flavors of Ethiopian confectionery with a global audience. Our dedication to excellence ensures that KAYO PLC will remain a cherished and respected name in the confectionery industry for years to come.

Join us in creating joyful moments with every bite. Choose Meaza, choose quality, choose KAYO.
